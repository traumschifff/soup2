research and webpublication • 2023 • a collaboration with Noam Youngrak Son

Show me Love

Can visual language of economic diagrams be redesigned to portray the more-than-human bodies and their reproduction not as exploitable resources but as loving subjects with agency? How will this influence the perception of the economic and reproductive subjectivity of more-than-humans?

As the duo Anguille Vannamei, Noam and I investigated economic diagrams, and created a manifesto for a hydrofeminist diagrammatic practice.

We published the results of our research under diagram.love.

The research has been generously supported by the Design Starting Grant of Stimulering Fonds.

The initiation phase of our duo was guided by the Underexposed mentoring program by Sonic Acts.

We also had the honour to be interviewed by the Ecoes Magazine #4.

video installation • 2023 • in collaboration with Miles Worner

Fanfiction Wishing Well

„Mantis Dance“ is a film that oscillates between states of embodied Femme Fatale and hyper sexual Subjectivity as well as dangerous, dreadful, hungry Mantis - a homicidal and cannibalistic creation.

I am happy to have a super cool team of collaborators on this project:

  • Performance & Concept by Marlene G. Prinz
  • Music by DJ Fuckoff and Chebedajha
  • Post-Production by Video Power

The footage was filmed in July 2022 at Bananen Club, a Strip Club in the Red Light District of Amsterdam.

Selections

  • Editing Pitch by Video Power (2023)
  • Visual Essay from filmstills published in Womanly Magazine and Screwmag NYC (2023)
  • Trailer presented at Unfinished Film Festival and at the FemGarage exhibition at Highbrow Institute Vienna (2023)
